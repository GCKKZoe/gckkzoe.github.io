I am a Ph.D. candidate in Finance at HKU Business School, The University of Hong Kong, and I will be on the 2026-2027 academic job market. My research focuses on financial intermediaries, information disclosure, equity research analysts, and institutions and financial markets in China.

Before starting the Ph.D., I worked at Ernst & Young Limited as a Staff Consultant. I received my B.B.A. in Accounting and Finance from The University of Hong Kong with First Class Honours and was a visiting student at the University of California, Berkeley.

You can find my current [CV here]({{ base_path }}/files/CV_Zhang_Chuyi.pdf) and a fuller list of papers on the [Research]({{ base_path }}/research/) page.
